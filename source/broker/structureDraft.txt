  Package structure:

  src/main/java/com/sismaguard/broker/
  ├── BrokerApplication.java              ← @SpringBootApplication
  ├── config/
  │   └── WebSocketConfig.java            ← registers /stream endpoint
  ├── dto/
  │   ├── SensorSummary.java              ← record from simulator
  │   ├── SensorMeasurement.java          ← record from simulator
  │   ├── Coordinates.java                ← nested record
  │   └── BrokerMessage.java             ← our enriched DTO
  ├── service/
  │   ├── SensorDiscoveryService.java     ← GET /api/devices/ at startup
  │   └── SimulatorConnectionService.java ← opens 12 WS to simulator
  └── handler/
  └── ReplicaWebSocketHandler.java    ← accepts replicas, broadcasts
